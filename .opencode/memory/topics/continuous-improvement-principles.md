## Continuous Improvement Principles
date:20260323
core_principles:
- kaizen: small, incremental changes compound over time
- pdca cycle: plan-do-check-act for systematic improvement
- retrospectives: regular reflection on what works and what doesn't
- feedback loops: fast feedback enables rapid adjustment
- experimentation: treat changes as experiments, not permanent commitments
key_practices:
- weekly reviews: assess what worked, what didn't, what to try next
- metric tracking: measure what matters to guide improvement efforts
- small batches: make small, frequent changes rather than large infrequent ones
- blameless postmortems: focus on learning, not assigning blame
- standardization: once improvement is validated, standardize the new way
application_areas:
- personal productivity and habit formation
- software development processes and code quality
- system administration and infrastructure maintenance
- learning and skill development
- relationships and communication
benefits:
- sustainable progress without burnout
- adaptability to changing circumstances
- cumulative advantage over time
- increased resilience and antifragility
